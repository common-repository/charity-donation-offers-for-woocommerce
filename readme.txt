Charity Donation Offers for WooCommerce

== Description ==

Add social impact to your online store and encourage greater customer spend.

Charity Donation Offers for Woocommerce is a simple, lightweight plugin that allows store owners to easily offer charity donations as an alternative to discounts or free shipping.

Choose a registered charity or cause to support then present donation offers to your customers based on how much they spend, encouraging them to spend more and benefit a charity at the same time.

For example, if your customers spend £50, you can pledge to make a donation of £5 to your local food bank.

At the back end, pay the donations to your chosen charity quickly and easily through Paypal, at the click of a button.

The plugin works seamlessly with Woocommerce.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1.Log in to your WordPress admin panel and go to Plugins -> Add New
2.Type Charity Donation Offers for WooCommerce in the search box and click on search button.
3.Find Charity Donation Offers for WooCommerce plugin.
4.Then click on Install Now after that activate the plugin.

== Screenshots ==
1. Add your chosen charity or cause as a custom product type in Woocommerce with logo and Paypal details
2. Configure a rule in Woocommerce settings e.g. If the cart subtotal is greater than £20, we will donate £2 to our chosen charity 
3. Write a message to show to customers and decide where to display it on product, cart and / or checkout pages
4. Customer sees the message and charity logo displayed on the front end
5. If customer spends more than the set amount, the charity donation pledge (made by the eCommerce store owner) is seen in the basket
6. From the Woocommerce orders screen easily send donations to the Charity via Paypal
